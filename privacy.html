Privacy Policy

CanopyMD respects your privacy and is committed to protecting user data.

Information We Collect

CanopyMD may collect plant images, optional grow environment details, and app usage information to provide plant health analysis and improve the user experience.

Image Processing

Images uploaded through the app may be processed by third-party AI analysis providers to generate plant health insights. Images are used solely for analysis purposes.

Personal Information

CanopyMD does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information. Users should avoid uploading content unrelated to plant analysis.

Data Security

Reasonable safeguards are used to help protect submitted information, though no system can guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

CanopyMD may rely on third-party infrastructure and AI providers to operate app functionality.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically as the app evolves.
